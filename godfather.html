The Godfather Story:

As the head of the New York Corleone mob family decides to pass on his position, a series of unfortunate events begin to unfold. A war bewtween the mob organization leads to murder and revenge until a family member of the Corleone family steps up.

  • Written by J. S. Golden
  • Director: Francis Ford Coppola
  • Release Date: 24 March 1972
